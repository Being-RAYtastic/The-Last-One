Player 1 Controls:
	a  = Move Left
	d  = Move Right
	w  = Jump
	Spacebar = Attack
	e  = Emotional Damage 

	Note: Make sure to turn OFF CapsLock


Player 2 Controls:
	ArrowLeft Key = Move Left
	ArrowRight Key = Move Right
	ArrowUp Key = Jump
	ArrowDown Key = Attack
	Enter Key = Emotional Damage



Note: If the browser isn't able to play the background music then go to your browser settings, then search AutoPlay and either:- 
	Enable Autoplay 
		or
	Paste the URL of the file/website in the Exceptions List
