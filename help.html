Player 1 Controls:

  • a = Move Left
  • d = Move Right
  • w = Jump
  • s/Spacebar = Attack
  • e = Emotional Damage


    • Note: Make sure to turn OFF CapsLock



Player 2 Controls:

  • ArrowLeft Key = Move Left
  • ArrowRight Key = Move Right
  • ArrowUp Key = Jump
  • ArrowDown Key = Attack
  • Enter Key = Emotional Damage


Note: Emotional Damage can be used only 1 time per player in a match



Note: If the browser isn't able to play the background music then go to your browser settings, then search AutoPlay and either:-

  1. Enable Autoplay
  2. Paste the URL of the file/website in the Exceptions List
